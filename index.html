About Auntie Betty

We expect the COVID vaccine for kids 0-4 to receive approval by mid-June. We know that
COVID-19 vaccination rate for kids 5-18 in the Black community in Toronto is already significantly lower compared to non-racialized communities. Without EASY access to credible and culturally safe information, Black parents will be susceptible to misinformation and be less likely to vaccinate with 0- 4-year-olds.

Auntie Betty is an easily accessible, culturally safe chatbot for Black parents to support shared decision-making in advance of 0-4 COVID vaccine approval. This chatbot is available on our dedicated website, as well as on community partner websites. The content will be Toronto-specific and include links to real providers who can support Black parentsâ€™ shared decision-making about vaccinating their kids.
